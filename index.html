How long does ceramic coating last?

+

Ceramic coating typically lasts between 1 to 5 years, depending on the coating package, driving conditions, and maintenance. It helps maintain gloss, water repellency, and surface protection over time.

Do I need to follow special care after installing PPF?

+

PPF does not require special care; however, gentle washing and proper maintenance help extend its lifespan. Avoid harsh chemicals and high-pressure washing near edges, especially during the first few days after installation.

Can ceramic or graphene coatings reduce scratches and swirl marks?

+

Yes. These coatings help minimize light scratches and swirl marks by adding a protective layer, but they are not designed to protect against deep scratches or severe impacts.

How many years does PPF typically last?

+

Paint Protection Film (PPF) typically lasts 5 to 10 years, depending on the film quality, driving conditions, and maintenance. Premium PPF is designed to resist yellowing, peeling, and cracking while protecting your car from scratches, stone chips, and environmental damage.
